# UX Strategy

Find information on the UX strategy and guiding principles that shape the way we do UX, design and development at FE fundinfo.

---

## Mission Statement

Accelerating value delivery through tools, systems and practices that improve speed and quality while reducing unnecessary reengineering.

## Vision Statement

Evolve to UX organization through a culture of UX and innovation by embracing infused UX design practices and fully optimized digital touchpoints.

---

## Experience Strategy

Move from principle‑based to managed user experiences as part of standard maturity models by advancing through:

* Defined tools and processes
* Visual consistency of experiences
* Optimized behaviours
* Streamlined workflows
* Effective measurements

### Address the Gaps

* **Human elements:** stakeholders, SMEs, designers, product teams
* **Informational elements:** usage data, research practices, product feedback

### Derive

* Design criteria
* Functionality
* Features
* Success metrics

## Focus Areas

### 1. Streamline the Basics

* Consolidate tooling for improved design‑delivery handoffs
* Reengineer process for clarity of phasing, flow and ownership
* Establish cross‑functional engagement foundations

### 2. Streamline Delivery

* Improve design service delivery
* Improve requirements & product delivery management
* Ensure upstream UX decision‑making
* Adopt Just‑In‑Time design delivery
* Adopt Agile through DesignOps

### 3. Streamline Research

* Use data analytics for insights
* Create research frameworks for discovery & usability testing
* Improve adoption of Design Thinking across the organization

### 4. Create Accelerators

* Build frameworks & toolkits
* Standardize design systems
* Build reusable frontend frameworks
* Improve performance baselines
* Manage design debt via catalogs
* Support teams with micro front‑ends & service layer structure

## Frontend Strategy

### Strategic Goals

* Decouple frontends for future development
* Adopt micro frontends
* Integrate Storybook, Bit, Mitosis
* Improve efficiency with automation & pattern libraries

### Requirements & Benefits

* Autonomy
* Scalability
* Single responsibility
* Reusability
* Tech agnostic approach
* Domain‑driven architecture
